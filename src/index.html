Thanks for checking in. Since the internet has changed flavors in so many ways lately, most of my moves to express myself have manifested as deleting profiles and content that Terms of Service agreements designate as belonging to someone else, or at the whims of whatever new policies. My privacy has become more important. I still want to share, on my own terms. I would like to meet other people in the middle on social platforms - but so far, it's websites (ol faithful) that still seem to be appropriately owned and private. I still am trying to figure out exactly what to do here with this space.
